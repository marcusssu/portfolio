About Mobify

Mobify (www.mobify.com) is the mobile shopping platform of choice for leading global retailers. We power shopping around the world through web, apps and brick and mortar stores.

Responsibilities

  • Make our products shine with world class visual design, user interaction, and animation
  • You'll create the experience, design the pixels, work with our engineering team to execute, and iterate frequently
  • Collaborate with cross discipline teams to prototype, design and code web-based experiences
  • Research mobile design patterns that work, test with users
  • Contribute to weekly design meetings and be receptive to feedback from other designers

skills/qualifications

  • Have a strong online portfolio showcasing user interface and experience design, along with code examples
  • Are familiar with all the latest responsive, mobile-first web design techniques and philosophies
  • Are able to quickly and efficiently hand-code your designs into HTML and CSS without the assistance of CSS frameworks like Bootstrap
  • Have a passion for UI and UX design, web and mobile
  • Are thoughtful in your design process and can communicate solutions to an engineering team
  • Take ownership over your work from start to finish
